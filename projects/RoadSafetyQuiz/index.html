Road Safety Tips

Speed Management

Always drive at a speed that allows you to stop within the distance you can see to be clear.

Safe Following Distance

Maintain a 3-second gap between you and the vehicle in front. Increase in poor conditions.

Sign Recognition

Regularly refresh your knowledge of traffic signs - they communicate important regulations.
